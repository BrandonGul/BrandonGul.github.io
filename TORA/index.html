TORA

TORA IS A MINIMAL REMINDER APP
IT USES TEXT TO MAKE A TIMER/ALARM SO CREATING A REMINDER IS ONE FULID MOVEMENT.

SET A ALARM FOR A SPACE LAUNCH!
TRY:
"SPACE LAUNCH AT ZERO FIVE HUNDRED HOURS"

HAVE A FANCY DINNER TO ATTEND? SET A ALARM!
HOW ABOUT:
"FANCY DINNER AROUND EIGHT REMIND ME AT SEVEN PM"

HAVE A SHOW YOU'RE BINGING AND IN NEED OF SOME DELICIOUS FOODS. REMIND YOURSELF!
YOU COULD GO WITH:
"ORDER FOOD TO GO PICK UP IN THIRTY FIVE MINUTES"

DON'T FORGET TO FEED MR. WHISKERS ;_;
WHY NOT SET A REMINDER:
"FEED MR WISKERS IN A HOUR AND A HALF"
THERE YOU GO...

CALL YOUR GRANDMA! SHE IS A WONDERFUL WOMAN SHE NEEDS ATTENTION!
TRY SOMETHING LIKE THIS:
"CALL GRANDMA IN 50 MINUTES"

REMIMBER TO LIMBER UP!
WRITE SOMETHING MOTIVATIONAL LIKE:
"DON'T BE LAZY IN 45 MINUTES"
